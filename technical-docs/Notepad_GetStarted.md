## ðŸ§­ How to Open Notepad

### Option 1: From the Start Menu
1. Click the **Start** button (or press the `Windows` key).

1. Press `Windows + R` to open the Run dialog.
2. Type `notepad` and press `Enter`.

## ðŸ“„ Creating and Saving a File

1. Open **Notepad**.
2. Begin typing your content.
3. To save the file:
   - Click **File > Save As**
   - Choose a location
   - Enter a **file name**
   - Select a **file extension** (e.g., `.txt`, `.html`, `.bat`)
   - Click **Save**

ðŸ“ Tip: Use quotes around the filename to preserve the extension, e.g., `"script.bat"`.

## âœï¸ Editing and Formatting
